Portfolio of Specialties

Maintenance & Comprehensive Engineering Services

Explore the catalog of solutions grouped by categories, designed to offer maximum clarity and technical accessibility.

Industrial & SMB Electricity

Design, installation, and maintenance of main and distribution electrical panels, laying of low and medium voltage cabling, certified groundings with earth spike measurement, efficient LED lighting, and power factor correction. Safe and stable electrical solutions custom-designed for shops, SMBs, and large factories.

Industrial & SMB Gas Systems

Installation, maintenance, and tightness tests in gas networks for SMBs and industries. Piping assemblies, regulatory ventilation, and installation & preventive maintenance of appliances and boilers are performed through certified gas technicians under strict Camuzzi safety guidelines.

Water Management & Plumbing

Commercial and industrial plumbing and water solutions. Drinking water networks, sewage, storm drains, and installation of cisterns with pressurized pumping systems to guarantee optimal water pressure. Services cover the installation of bathroom fixtures, countertops, and sinks, as well as automated underground and sprinkler irrigation systems for gardens and parks.

Pneumatics & Compressed Air

Design, installation, and maintenance of pneumatic compressors and compressed air distribution networks (pipes and ducts). Pressure and flow rate are optimized for the correct operation of machinery and pneumatic tools—indispensable in workshops, building sites, and industrial plants—ensuring maximum efficiency without power loss or energy waste.

Painting, Coatings & Surface Treatment

Anticorrosive protection treatment for metallic structures, tanks, and machinery, combined with professional interior and exterior painting for SMBs and industries. Textured coatings (Revear), synthetics, latex, satin, and high-resistance varnishes are applied on brickwork, metal/wood openings, ceilings, fronts, retail stores, and warehouses. Specialization is also available in high-traffic commercial flooring, self-leveling epoxy floors, and high-washability technical paints.

Structural Integrity & Roof Repair

Diagnosis, reinforcement, and repair of roofs (concrete slabs, sheets, or tiles), columns, and foundations. Leaks, drips, and structural cracks are resolved by applying liquid or asphalt membranes, along with custom sheet metal flashing (gutters and downspouts) to protect the safety and value of the warehouse, industrial plant, or retail store.

Data Networks, Telecoms & Connectivity

Certified installation of data networks: UTP (Cat 5e and Cat 6) and Fiber Optic for SMBs and industrial sites. Standard Wi-Fi networks and high-coverage Mesh systems are designed and implemented, and complete data racks are assembled with switches, patch panels, cable organizers, and UPS uninterruptible power supplies. Setup of servers, conventional/IP telephone switchboards, and local/cloud backup systems is also managed.

Security, Video Surveillance & Alarms

Installation of comprehensive security systems for SMBs and industries. CCTV circuits and AI smart cameras (UTP/Wi-Fi) are designed and set up with autonomous event tracking (premium lines like Dahua Imou, TP-Link Tapo, Hikvision Ezviz), enabling real-time smartphone monitoring. Advanced intrusion alarms and certified fire detection systems (PCI) are also configured via optical sensors and infrared barriers.

Automation & Smart Home · Integration Engineering

No generic devices are sold: intelligent systems are programmed to exact specifications. N3i develops proprietary platforms based on local servers running Home Assistant, integrated via Python scripts that unify into a single robust interface: perimeter security, zone lighting, multi-zone climate control, energy management, and access control. Unlike cloud-based commercial systems, N3i's architecture guarantees full data privacy, offline operation during internet outages, and scalability without external licensing dependencies. The result: an adaptive system that grows with your operation, from a single-family home to a residential complex or industrial plant.

Renewable Energy & Backup Generation

Design, installation, and maintenance of advanced power generation systems and renewable energies for SMBs, households, and industries. Emergency backup generators (gas, gasoline, or diesel) and solar PV systems are deployed and adapted to each need: **On-Grid** (grid-tied for saving by net-metering), **Off-Grid** (100% autonomous isolated systems with battery banks), and **Hybrid** (smart backup balance). Uninterrupted power supply is guaranteed during grid outages, optimizing costs and eco-footprint.

Civil Engineering & Architecture

Planning, structural design, and technical management of civil works and commercial architecture. Projects cover everything from earthworks, grading, and foundations, to the calculation and assembly of metallic structures (roofs and industrial sheds), reinforced concrete slabs, turnkey corporate offices, and commercial stores, managing every stage under strict schedules, budgets, and safety regulations.

Steel Frame, Wood Frame & Drywall

Drywall construction and architecture under **Steel Frame** and **Wood Frame** standards for residential, retail, and industrial use. Design and execution cover everything from turnkey homes and commercial facilities to interior remodeling, lightweight extensions, and ceiling systems (joint-finish or 60x60 lay-in). Work is carried out with market-leading brands (Durlock, Knauf, Barbieri) using structural profiles and specialized gypsum boards (standard ST, green moisture-resistant WR, red fire-resistant FR, or Superboard cement boards for external cladding), ensuring rapid building speed and a superior thermal insulation that saves up to 40% in energy bills.

Industrial & Structural Metalwork & Custom Sheet Metal

Manufacturing, assembly, and maintenance of custom metal structures for industries and SMBs. Heavy and light metalwork is performed, including automated gates, railings, security grilles, industrial stairs, walkways, and platforms. In addition, complete solutions are offered in general sheet metal work (zinguería), designing and installing gutters, downspouts, flashings, spiro pipes for chimneys, ventilation ducts, and metal enclosures to guarantee proper rainwater drainage and airtightness of roofs, industrial warehouses, and commercial spaces.
