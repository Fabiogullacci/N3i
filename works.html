Our Recent Works

Explore some of the projects and maintenance jobs we have completed, guaranteeing quality and durability in every detail.

Sheet Metal & Structural Repair

Rainwater Drainage System Renovation

Zelarrayán 33 St., Bahía Blanca

Comprehensive replacement and repair of an old rainwater drainage system that showed a high degree of deterioration. Damaged components were removed and high-resistance waterproofing sealant was applied, ensuring proper drainage during heavy rains and definitively protecting the building's structure.

Electrical Inspection & Certification

Commercial Technical Review (85Kva)

Chiclana 280 St., Bahía Blanca

Comprehensive electrical technical inspection of a commercial premises for the change of ownership of a Three-Phase Electrical Meter with an installed power of 85Kva. Panels, protections, and circuits were surveyed according to current regulations, preparing and managing the mandatory technical documentation for the issuance of the Electrical Aptitude Certificate. The procedure was fully prepared for its correct presentation and endorsement before the College of Technicians and the Electromechanics Department of the Municipality of Bahía Blanca.

Comprehensive Construction & Maintenance

Industrial Warehouse Restoration

Alem 2780 Ave., Bahía Blanca

Execution of a comprehensive restoration and refurbishment project for an industrial warehouse. The work included dismantling obsolete structures, surface preparation, and general painting for both interiors and exteriors. Additionally, an exhaustive technical review and standardization of the electrical circuits were carried out, guaranteeing the safety, efficiency, and full regulatory compliance of the warehouse's entire electrical installation.

Drywall Construction & Finishings

Single-Family Home Remodeling

19 de Mayo St., Bahía Blanca

Comprehensive renovation of interior spaces applying dry construction systems. The structure and dropping of ceilings were carried out using high-strength structural Steel Frame profiles. The project included the design and assembly of modern partitions, installation of top-performance aluminum openings, and the mounting of transparent tempered glass walls to maximize spatial luminosity. As seen in the record, puttying and fine sanding finishes were executed, and the complete protective paint scheme is currently being applied to the upper surfaces.
